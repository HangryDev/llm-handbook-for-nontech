# AI Systems Platform
## Product Overview

Version: Draft v1  
Date: March 2026

---

# 1. Product Vision

The AI Systems Platform enables professionals to learn how **real AI systems are designed and used in practice**.

Most AI education focuses on tools or theory.

This platform focuses on **applied AI systems**.

Users should be able to:

- discover real AI systems
- understand how they work
- apply them to their own work
- eventually design their own systems

The platform develops **AI Power Users** and surfaces **AI Champions** who design high-impact systems.

---

# 2. Product Philosophy

The product is built on several principles.

### Applied AI First

The focus is real systems used in real work.

Examples include:

- agent systems
- automation architectures
- multi-step AI pipelines
- domain-specific AI systems

---

### Experts Create Knowledge

AI Champions design advanced systems.

Operators extract and structure their insights into usable knowledge.

---

### Systems Over Tools

AI tools change rapidly.

The durable knowledge lies in **system design and operational thinking**.

---

### Continuous Learning

AI evolves quickly.

The platform must continuously publish new systems and insights.

---

# 3. Product Positioning

The platform sits between several existing categories.

Traditional categories:

- AI courses
- AI tool directories
- AI news platforms
- developer documentation

This product combines elements of all of them, but centers on **AI systems**.

Positioning:

Applied AI Systems Platform

---

# 4. Core Product Model

The product revolves around three elements.

AI Champions  
↓  
AI Systems  
↓  
Knowledge Distribution

Champions design systems.

Operators convert expert insights into structured knowledge.

Members learn and apply these systems.

---

# 5. Core Product Components

The platform consists of four main layers.

---

## AI Systems Library

The core asset of the platform.

A repository of real AI systems designed by experts.

Each system includes:

- problem definition
- system architecture
- tools used
- implementation steps
- case study

Users browse and learn from these systems.

---

## Knowledge Layer

Structured knowledge surrounding AI systems.

Examples:

- architecture breakdowns
- tool evaluations
- implementation guides
- system design principles

Over time this evolves into a **knowledge graph of applied AI systems.**

---

## Learning Layer

Educational programs built around real AI systems.

Formats include:

- lectures
- workshops
- guided exercises
- system walkthroughs

Learning content is derived from Champion insights.

---

## Community Layer

A collaborative environment where members:

- discuss systems
- explore tools
- share experiments
- discover new ideas

Community interaction helps surface new insights.

---

# 6. User Types

The product serves three primary user types.

---

## Members

Professionals who want to apply AI in their work.

Examples:

- consultants
- marketers
- founders
- researchers

Goal:

Learn and apply practical AI systems.

---

## AI Power Users

Advanced practitioners who experiment with systems.

Typical activities:

- modifying AI systems
- experimenting with tools
- testing new architectures

Goal:

Develop deeper expertise in applied AI.

---

## AI Champions

Experts who design high-impact AI systems.

Their outputs include:

- agentic systems
- automation architectures
- domain-specific AI systems
- applied case studies

Their work feeds the platform’s knowledge base.
