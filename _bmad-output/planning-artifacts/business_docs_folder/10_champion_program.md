# AI Systems Platform
## Champion Program

Version: Draft v1  
Date: March 2026

---

# 1. Program Purpose

The Champion Program identifies and supports individuals who design high-impact **AI systems**.

Champions are not general educators.

They are **experts who create powerful applied AI systems within their domain**.

The purpose of the program is to:

- discover high-level practitioners
- capture expert knowledge
- publish impactful AI systems
- elevate the overall capability of the ecosystem

Champions are the primary creators of the platform's most valuable knowledge.

---

# 2. Role of an AI Champion

AI Champions are domain experts who design and apply AI systems to real problems.

They are recognized for the **impact and originality of their systems**, not for teaching volume or community participation.

Champions primarily focus on:

designing AI systems  
experimenting with tools and architectures  
solving real-world problems  
publishing high-impact case studies

They represent the **expert layer of the ecosystem**.

---

# 3. What Champions Do

Champions are expected to contribute in the following ways.

### System Creation

Design and implement original **AI systems**.

Examples:

research synthesis systems  
marketing automation systems  
decision-support systems  
knowledge management systems

---

### Insight Sharing

Share the underlying design thinking behind their systems.

This knowledge becomes the foundation for:

- lectures
- workshops
- documentation
- AI systems library

---

### Optional Lectures

Champions may choose to give lectures or workshops.

This is optional and not required.

Teaching is not the core responsibility of a Champion.

---

# 4. What Champions Do NOT Do

Champions are not required to:

mentor new students  
run community support  
answer beginner questions  
provide technical support

Those responsibilities belong to the platform operators and learning programs.

Champions focus on **high-level knowledge creation**.

---

# 5. Knowledge Extraction Process

Champion insights are transformed into educational assets through operator collaboration.

The process works as follows.

Champion builds AI system  
↓  
Operator interviews Champion  
↓  
System architecture documented  
↓  
AI system published in library  
↓  
Lecture or workshop created

This allows Champions to focus on expertise while operators handle knowledge production.

---

# 6. Champion Promotion

Promotion to Champion status is determined by the company.

Evaluation is based on the **impact of published knowledge**, including:

quality of AI systems  
originality of ideas  
real-world usefulness  
community influence

Contribution volume alone does not determine promotion.

Impact and insight are the primary criteria.

---

# 7. Champion Visibility

Champions gain visibility through:

AI system publications  
case studies  
lectures and workshops  
featured content  
community recognition

Their work becomes part of the platform's core knowledge base.

This builds professional reputation for the Champion.

---

# 8. Revenue Participation

Champions may participate in revenue generated from their knowledge.

Possible revenue sources include:

lectures  
workshops  
advanced courses  
AI system deep dives  
enterprise training

Revenue sharing structures are defined per program.

Example model:

Champion lecture revenue  
50–70% to Champion  
30–50% to platform

Exact terms depend on the specific program.

---

# 9. Elite Model

The Champion Program follows an **elite model**, not a mass contributor model.

The goal is to identify a small number of highly capable individuals who produce exceptional systems.

Typical scale

5–10 Champions in early stage

This ensures high quality and strong reputation for the platform.

---

# 10. Strategic Importance

Champions are the foundation of the ecosystem.

They generate the most valuable assets:

AI systems  
case studies  
architectural insights  
applied techniques

These assets power the platform's:

education  
content  
community growth  
industry authority

Without Champions, the platform becomes a standard education provider.

With Champions, it becomes a **center of applied AI expertise**.

---

# 11. Long-Term Vision

Over time, Champions form a network of recognized experts.

Their published systems create a living knowledge library.

The platform becomes known as the place where **the best applied AI systems are developed and shared**.

Champions are at the center of this ecosystem.
